A Camera Kit for Digital Solargraphy

Build your own ultra-long exposure camera for Digital Solargraphy and Timelapses

Capture multi-day long exposures

Running on the minimum of two 18650 batteries, the camera can take images for more than two days. Fully loaded with 6 batteries, up to a week is possible.
